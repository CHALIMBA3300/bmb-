Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days within Lusaka, and 5-7 business days to other parts of Zambia.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with packaging.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes, all products come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranties available on select items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Money), and bank transfers.
